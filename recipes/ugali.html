African Ugali

Description

In Kenya,Ugali is the most loved meal. This is the list of ingredients and how it is prepared.

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg Maize flour
  • 2 cups of water

Procedure

  1. Heat the water until its starts to boil
  2. Add small amount of flour to the boiling water as you stir using a cooking stick
  3. Add flour after stirring and again stir. Repeat this until the ugali is hard enough. Leave the ugali to be heated for about 1 minute. Put your ugali in a plate.

    4. Ugali in Kenya often accompanies vegetables,fried eggs,beef... etc
